2-Component Waterproofing Systems

Our 2-component waterproofing systems are engineered to provide superior protection for a wide range of surfaces. Combining advanced polymers and hardeners, these systems offer exceptional durability, flexibility, and waterproofing performance, making them ideal for both residential and commercial applications.

Key Features
  • Superior Waterproofing: Creates a robust barrier against water infiltration, ensuring complete protection for your surfaces.
  • Enhanced Durability: Resistant to cracking, peeling, and abrasion, offering long-lasting performance.
  • Flexibility: Adapts to structural movements, preventing damage and maintaining integrity.
  • Chemical Resistance: Protects surfaces from the damaging effects of chemicals and environmental factors.

Ideal Applications

  • Foundations and Basements: Prevents water ingress, protecting structural integrity and indoor air quality.

  • Roofing: Provides a durable, weather-resistant barrier that extends the lifespan of your roof.

  • Balconies and Terraces: Ensures waterproofing in outdoor areas exposed to weather conditions.

  • Bathrooms and Wet Areas: Protects against moisture damage in high-humidity environments.

  • Swimming Pools and Water Features: Offers reliable waterproofing for submerged surfaces.

Benefits

  • Long-Lasting Protection: Extends the life of surfaces by providing a strong, waterproof barrier against damage.

  • Versatile Solutions: Suitable for both new constructions and renovation projects.

  • Cost-Effective: Reduces the need for frequent repairs and maintenance, saving you time and money in the long run.

  • Improved Aesthetics: Provides a smooth, seamless finish that enhances the appearance of your surfaces.

Application Guidelines

  • Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free of dust, grease, and loose material. Repair any significant cracks or damage.

  • Mixing: Combine the two components according to the manufacturerâ€™s instructions, mixing thoroughly to achieve a uniform consistency.

  • Application: Apply the mixture using a brush, roller, or spray equipment. For best results, apply multiple coats, allowing each coat to dry fully before applying the next.

  • Curing: Allow the waterproofing system to cure as per the specified drying times. Avoid heavy use or water exposure until fully cured.

  • Finishing Touches: Apply any desired topcoats or sealers for additional protection and finish.
