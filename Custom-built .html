Custom-Built Formulas

At Sigma Formulations, we understand that every project is unique and may require specialized solutions. Our custom-built formulas are designed to meet your specific needs, providing tailored performance, durability, and aesthetic appeal. Whether you need a unique coating for a challenging environment or a specialized finish for a high-profile project, our custom formulations offer the perfect solution.

Key Features
  • Tailored Solutions: Each custom-built formula is designed to address the specific requirements of your project, ensuring optimal performance.
  • High Performance: Our custom formulations offer superior durability, flexibility, and resistance to environmental factors.
  • Versatile Applications: Suitable for a wide range of applications, including industrial, commercial, and residential projects.
  • Quality Assurance: Every custom-built formula undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets the highest standards of quality and performance.


  • Benefits

Benefits

  • Personalized Approach: Get a coating solution that is specifically designed to address your unique challenges and requirements.

  • Aesthetic Flexibility: Achieve the exact look and finish you desire, with customizable colors, textures, and effects.

  • Cost Efficiency: Tailored solutions can help reduce long-term maintenance and repair costs by providing better performance and longevity.

  • Sustainability Options: We offer eco-friendly custom formulations that meet your sustainability goals, including low VOC and recyclable options.

Application Areas

  • Architectural Finishes: Unique coatings for residential and commercial buildings, providing both aesthetic appeal and functional benefits.

  • Specialty Projects: Tailored solutions for specialized applications such as heritage buildings, art installations, and more.

  • Marine and Protective Coatings: Formulas specifically designed to withstand harsh marine environments and provide robust protection.

Process

  • Consultation: We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your specific needs, project requirements, and desired outcomes.

  • Research and Development: Our team of experts conducts extensive research and development to create a formula that meets your specifications.

  • Prototype and Testing: We develop prototypes and conduct rigorous testing to ensure the formula performs as expected in real-world conditions.

  • Production: Once the formula is finalized, we produce the custom coating in our state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring consistent quality and performance.

Support and Resources

  • Expert Advice: Our team of chemists and technical experts is available to provide personalized consultations and support throughout your project.

  • Educational Resources: Access our library of case studies, white papers, and technical datasheets for in-depth information on our custom-built formulas.

  • Ongoing Assistance: We offer ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure your custom coatings continue to perform at their best.
